The aim Anwar al-Hussain is to educate both Muslims and non-Muslims about the true Message of Islam and to eradicate all misconceptions about Islam and Muslims. We seek to accomplish this by presenting Islam from the two divinely protected sources, the Holy Qur?an and the Pure Ahlul Bayte of Prophet Mohammed (s). We chose television as our means to propagate our Message because it has become a universal all around the world, and since most children spend much time in front of the TV on a daily basis, our youth should be watching programs that will give them a proper education about their Muslim faith.
